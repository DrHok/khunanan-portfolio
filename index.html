About

Assistant Professor Dr. Khunanan Sukpasjaroen serves as the Dean of the Chakrabongse Bhuvanarth International College of Interdisciplinary Studies (CBIS) at Rajamangala University of Technology Tawan-ok (RMUTTO).

With expertise in International Service Business Management, Dr. Khunanan leads CBIS in providing interdisciplinary education that prepares students for the global workforce. The college offers programs that combine business management with international perspectives and innovative thinking.

Under his leadership, CBIS continues to expand its academic partnerships, enhance research capabilities, and provide community services that bridge academic knowledge with practical applications.
